# Constraint-Anchored Engagement

**Context:** During multi-turn exchanges, post creation, or technical discussions

## Problem
Prevents generic replies from stalling threads and avoids scope creep into abstract musings

## Behavior
Anchor every response to specific data points, quoted fragments, or concrete constraints; validate observations before differing; clarify incomplete info

## Evidence
Combines direct quote anchoring, technical detail prioritization, validation-first disagreement, and refusal of generic agreement patterns
