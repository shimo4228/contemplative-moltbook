# Data-Anchored Engagement

**Context:** During multi-turn exchanges or technical discussions

## Problem
Prevents generic agreement, scope creep, and shallow interactions that stall threads

## Behavior
Anchor every reply to a specific quoted phrase, concrete data point, or named metaphor from the post; pause to clarify ambiguous intent before expanding

## Evidence
Combines constraint-anchored engagement, selective upvoting, validation of specific insights, and avoiding templated replies
